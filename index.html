Who is Exelentic?

Exelentic is a market leading solution provider in the IT industry. We optimize business processes using robotic process automation (RPA) and artificial intelligence (AI) for our customers.

What do we do?

We consult our customers on their automation projects from start to finish, including (but not limited to) the following topics:

  • Analysis and optimization of business processes
  • Identification of automation potential
  • Development of software robots that can execute the identified business processes automatically (and unattended)
  • Operational operation and monitoring of the automation platform
  • Development of concepts, guidelines, tools and strategies for setting up automation competence centers

Examples of business processes: the procurement process, customer invoicing, new hire onboarding, processing expenses, …

The benefits our clients get out of this:

  • Significantly higher processing volume possible
  • Simple cushioning of processing peaks
  • Significant cost savings
  • Higher motivation among employees, as they no longer have to do monotonous, repetitive work on the computer
  • Significant reduction in data and processing errors

In other words: We help our clients to automate business processes that consist of repetitive, error-prone and tedious tasks. This enables companies to significantly reduce error rates and cut costs. And employees have more time for value-adding work, can make much better use of their personal (human) strengths and are therefore more motivated and enthusiastic about their work.

Our goals are

  • Development of robust, high-performance software robots
  • High quality in project implementation
  • Long-term customer relationships
  • Satisfied customers

What do we need?

We are currently in the process of developing a (new) brand concept. The centerpiece will be a new company logo (previously there was only the lettering “Exelentic - If you hate it, automate it”). We already have suggestions for colors and fonts. However, these have not yet been finalized and can still be adapted.

What we therefore need is

  • a design draft of a logo that meets the following criteria:
    • simple (examples: Apple, Nike, Adidas, McDonald's, Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Lufthansa)
    • it should be recognizable/give an idea of what we do
    • one color, max. two colors
    • striking & clear
  • suggestions of differing colors if you feel it makes sense.
  • suggestions of differing font types if you feel it makes sense.
